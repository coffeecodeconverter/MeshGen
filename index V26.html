Guide & Instructions

What is this?
A parametric generator for creating animated, low-poly mesh backgrounds for websites and apps.

1. Design: Use the sliders to adjust the "Drift" (how much points move), "Speed", and "Filters" to find a look you like.

2. Export: Click "Export Background" and choose Download Project Engine. This gives you a standalone .html file ready for use.

3. Syncing: Already have a file? You don't need to download it again. Just click Copy Settings Only and paste that line into your existing project to update the visuals instantly.

Mesh Generator

A fully parametric, low-poly morphing background generator. Open settings to customize the drift, flow speed, and visual filters.
